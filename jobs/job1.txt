What you’ll be doing?
You’ll be working on core quality engineering problems across our Centres of Excellence. Each Centre balances the dual of Framework Development (The Ideal) and Business Enablement (The Practical).

1) Test Automation & Framework Engineering:
** Designing and building end-to-end automation frameworks that test ride booking flows, driver allocation, payments, and multi-modal transit across cities.
** Writing custom linters, static analysis rules, and compiler flags that catch bugs at the source—before code even reaches a test environment.
** Building automation scripts that integrate seamlessly into CI/CD pipelines, enabling continuous verification of every commit.
** Creating reusable test libraries and DSLs that empower other engineers to write tests effortlessly.

2) AI-Powered Quality Intelligence:
** Leveraging AI to generate test cases, identify regression hotspots, and predict failure-prone areas of the codebase.
** Building intelligent test selection systems that run the right tests at the right time, dramatically reducing feedback loops.
** Developing self-healing test infrastructure that adapts to UI and API changes without manual intervention.
** Exploring code-aware verification—understanding business logic deeply enough to write tests that go beyond surface-level validation.

3) Platform & Release Quality:
** Owning quality across mobile (Android & iOS) and backend platforms for a multi-tenant, multi-city application serving lakhs of rides daily.
** Ensuring reliability across diverse transit modes—auto-rickshaws, cabs, rentals, intercity (Bharat Taxi), and public transport (Chennai One).
** Driving shift-left testing practices—embedding quality gates early in the development lifecycle.
** Conducting root cause analysis on production incidents and building automated regression suites that prevent recurrence.

4) Infrastructure & Observability:
** Building test infrastructure that scales—parallel execution, device farms, and environment orchestration.
** Developing quality dashboards and observability tooling that give real-time insight into system health.
** Contributing to infrastructure-as-code practices for reproducible test environments.

Apply if you are:
** Passionate about quality engineering and see it as a craft—not just a checkbox
** Someone who reads code for breakfast. You understand systems deeply and can reason about where they’ll break.
** Excited by the intersection of AI and testing—you want to build the future of software verification
** A builder at heart—you’d rather create a framework than run the same manual test twice
** Curious about functional programming, DSLs, and compiler-level quality checks
** Someone who can stick to a project for long stretches of time either to solve a hard problem or to achieve a high bar of perfection (marathoner)
** An ambitious and constant learner who takes on hard challenges
