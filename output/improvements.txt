*   **Remove "Basic" Proficiencies:** Never label your skills as "basic" or "intermediate." Let your bullet points prove your level.
*   **Re-frame the "AWS Community" Role:** Since you lack a formal internship, frame this as **Technical Leadership & Open Source Contribution**. Focus on the *architecting* of demos rather than the *organizing* of workshops.
*   **Upgrade Project Architecture:** Move from "built a website" to "architected a distributed system." Mention things like Caching, WebSockets, and Schema Design.
*   **The "Testing" Mandate:** Add Jest, Cypress, or Mocha. A Tier-1 firm will not hire an SDE who doesn't mention automated testing.
*   **Add Academic Pedigree:** Even if your school isn't "Top Tier," include your GPA (if >3.5), relevant coursework (Distributed Systems, OS), and your Graduation Month/Year.
